MAP NOTES PHOTOS SETUP

Hi, I'm Josh, a 29-year-old former director of engineering/all-around internet/product/software guy from Portland, Oregon. Karen (my motorcycle) and I are currently on a trip around the US of A. We'll be riding down backroads, forest roads, gravel roads, and hopefully no snow roads, camping wherever we find a good view and no crowds.

If you're wondering, the map on this site is a custom Google Maps map that pulls in data from the Spot API that's hooked up to my Gen3 device. I plan to have it on tracking mode most waking hours, with near real-time updates to this site (every ~15mins). You'll also see photos on the map, which are coming from Instagram and placed at the coordinates for which they were taken (also updating every ~15mins).

As you can probably guess from the list of gear/upgrades/doo-dads below, I've been getting ready for this trip over the last 3+ months and anticipate it'll take me ~10k miles around the country over the next 4 months. If you fancy riding together for a bit, grabbing a beer or coffee, or willing to trade trip stories for a warm shower, send me an email at joshellington@gmail.com or shoot me a text at 503-536-3309.
